    About

    Gunita is a web-based photo album designed for couples to beautifully capture, organize, and relive their most meaningful memories together. With a romantic and minimalist aesthetic, it offers a heartfelt space to preserve special moments, from first dates to anniversaries. Turning shared experiences into a timeless digital keepsake.
